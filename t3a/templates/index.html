The Three Astronauts: The Race to Mars
Opera Out of Bounds…
Based on the book by Umberto Eco and Eugenio Carmi

The United States, Russia, and China are caught in a 21st-century space race, each vying to be the first nation to put an explorer on the surface of Mars.

The thrilling story ensues as humanity races toward the unknown …

Follow three astronauts as they evolve from competitors to allies, explore their common humanity, and reach across an abyss of differences...
